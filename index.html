Frequently Asked Questions

Everything About AI Readiness for Medical Practices

How AI search works for doctors — answered plainly.

What is an AI Readiness Score for a medical practice?
An AI Readiness Score measures how visible your medical practice is to AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. It grades your site across SEO, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), E-E-A-T signals, and AI crawler access — giving you an A–F letter grade with specific action items.
Why is my medical practice not showing up in ChatGPT?
Most practices are invisible to ChatGPT because they lack structured data (schema markup), have accidentally blocked AI crawlers like GPTBot in their robots.txt, or are missing E-E-A-T trust signals like medical disclaimers and physician credentials. Our free scanner diagnoses all of these in seconds.
How do I get my practice cited by AI search engines?
Your practice needs: (1) Physician and LocalBusiness schema markup, (2) E-E-A-T signals including credentials and medical disclaimers, (3) AI crawlers like GPTBot and Google-Extended allowed in robots.txt, and (4) GEO signals including Open Graph tags and FAQ content. Run your free scan to see exactly where you stand.
What is GEO — Generative Engine Optimization?
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your website to be cited by AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity AI, and Google AI Overviews. Unlike traditional SEO, GEO focuses on structured data, schema markup, authoritative content, and ensuring AI crawlers can access your site.
Is the AI Readiness Scanner really free?
Yes — 100% free, forever. No credit card, no signup required. Enter your domain and get your full AI Readiness Report with 40+ checks across SEO, GEO, E-E-A-T, and AI crawler access in about 15 seconds.
What does E-E-A-T mean for my practice website?
E-E-A-T stands for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trust — Google’s quality framework for medical websites. A strong E-E-A-T score means visible physician credentials, a medical disclaimer, privacy policy, patient reviews, and a complete About page. These signals are critical for both Google rankings and being cited by AI tools.
